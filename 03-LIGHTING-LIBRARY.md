# 03 — Lighting Library (8 Pre-Built Scenarios)

Copy-paste these lighting blocks directly into your prompts. Each one overrides Nano Banana Pro's default "Rembrandt" studio lighting.

---

## 1. Morning Window — Direct Sun
**Best for:** Optimizer, Progress Tracker archetypes
**Mood:** Energetic, sharp, awake

```json
"lighting": "Direct, harsh morning sunlight hitting face at 45-degree angle. High-contrast terminator line across the nose. Deep, hard-edged shadows. Translucent subsurface scattering illuminating edge of ear. 4500K temperature."
```

---

## 2. Morning Window — Overcast/Diffused
**Best for:** Wellness Mom, Glow-Up Girl archetypes
**Mood:** Soft, approachable, calm

```json
"lighting": "Diffused soft overcast morning light from large window. Wraparound lighting with ultra-soft shadow transitions. Cool 6500K color temperature, flat natural contrast."
```

---

## 3. Kitchen Mixed (Highest Converting for UGC)
**Best for:** Wellness Mom, Skeptic, any testimonial angle
**Mood:** Authentic, real-life, relatable

```json
"lighting": "Mixed color temperatures. Warm tungsten overhead recessed kitchen LEDs (3000K) casting downward shadows under brow, mixed with cool daylight (5500K) spilling from side window. Dual color temperature on skin."
```

---

## 4. Bathroom Vanity
**Best for:** Glow-Up Girl, beauty/collagen products
**Mood:** Raw, unfiltered, honest

```json
"lighting": "Harsh, unflattering top-down bathroom vanity LED lighting (4000K). Steep downward light angle creating deep raccoon shadows under brow bone and sharp shadow under nose. High greasy specular glare on forehead."
```

---

## 5. Outdoor Shade
**Best for:** Weekend Warrior, casual outdoor settings
**Mood:** Natural, relaxed, easy

```json
"lighting": "Open shade under a tree. Cool ambient blue sky illumination. Soft multi-directional bounce light, lack of directional shadows. Slight green color cast from foliage."
```

---

## 6. Outdoor Overcast
**Best for:** Any archetype, most forgiving lighting
**Mood:** Neutral, even, everyday

```json
"lighting": "Flat outdoor overcast lighting. Shadowless illumination, muted colors. White blown-out sky background. Catchlights reflecting the entire gray sky."
```

---

## 7. Indoor Evening — Lamps
**Best for:** Sleep, stress, wind-down products
**Mood:** Warm, intimate, tired

```json
"lighting": "Dim indoor ambient lighting. Warm 2700K floor lamp illumination. High ISO sensor noise, muddy shadows, loss of fine detail due to low-light smartphone noise reduction."
```

---

## 8. Car Interior
**Best for:** Busy Parent, on-the-go moments, commuter context
**Mood:** Candid, caught-in-the-moment, real

```json
"lighting": "Enclosed car interior physics. Strong, diffused directional daylight from driver-side window. Subtle cyan tint from automotive UV glass. Soft bounce light illuminating chin from gray dashboard below. Deep crushed shadows on opposite side."
```

---

## Choosing the Right Lighting

| Product Category | Best Lighting | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Greens / Daily Vitamins | Kitchen Mixed (#3) | Most relatable, "morning routine" feel |
| Protein / Pre-Workout | Morning Direct Sun (#1) | Sharp, energetic, "active lifestyle" |
| Collagen / Beauty | Bathroom Vanity (#4) or Overcast (#2) | Raw honesty or soft flattery |
| Sleep / Stress | Evening Lamps (#7) | Wind-down mood, warm intimate |
| Energy / Recovery | Morning Overcast (#2) | Fresh start, calm energy |
| Gut Health | Kitchen Mixed (#3) | Digestive = kitchen context |
| Meal Replacement | Kitchen Mixed (#3) or Car (#8) | Routine or on-the-go |
| Joint / Outdoor Active | Outdoor Shade (#5) | Active lifestyle, natural |
